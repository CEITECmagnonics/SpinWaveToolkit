SpinWaveToolkit.ObjectiveLens#

class SpinWaveToolkit.ObjectiveLens(wavelength, NA, f0, f)#

Represents an objective lens with specific optical parameters.

Module for calculating the electric field focused by an objective lens. Calculations follows the method presented in book of Novotny and Hecht.

Parameters:
wavelengthfloat

  1. wavelength of the light.

NAfloat

Numerical aperture of the objective lens.

f0float

Filling factor.

ffloat

  1. focal length of the objective lens.

Attributes:
same as Parameters
